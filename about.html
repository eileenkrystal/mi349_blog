Hi, my name is Eileen! I’m from Troy, Michigan and I am a sophomore at Michigan State University studying computer science. Through the connections I’ve made at MSU, I learned about UX/UI design and I’ve started to become more passionate about learning how to design interfaces that are usable, clear, and aesthetically pleasing.

Besides my interest in UI design, my other interests include:

  • photography
  • cooking
  • playing the violin

Photography is something that I really enjoy doing in my spare time. My interest in scenic photography started when I was around twelve years old. I started to cook more during the pandemic. Some of my favorite dishes that I’ve made include chicken noodle soup, fried rice, and fettuccine alfredo. In addition, I’ve been playing the violin since I was seven years old. My elementary school had the chance to teach students how to play the violin in first grade and I've loved it ever since.
